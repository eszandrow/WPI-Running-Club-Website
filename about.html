About Us

Our club was founded in Spring of 2013. The purpose of the Running Club is to bring together WPI students who are interested in running and provide an environment which supports personal growth and camaraderie. We create a social and constructive atmosphere with regular practices and meetings.

Mailing List

The mailing list is a low traffic way of staying notified about weekly running plans. Please email the officers at running-execs@wpi.edu to be added to the list.
